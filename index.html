Why We Formed

The Single Pringles Organization formed because of the many, many, many people that get into relationships at a very early age. It's a club to help support all the single beans out there and have fun without being nasty and making eye contact or something horrendous. We hold fun parties, watch shrek, eat ice cream, and make this club seem overly professional. We hope to keep this club going until we're a bit more mature and ready for relationships. Until then, we'll hold fun parties and do fantastic things.
